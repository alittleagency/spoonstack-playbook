LOCKED RULES
voice + copy rules
✦
spoonstack always lowercase — in code, copy, UI labels, database values, routes. No exceptions. Never capitalized.
✦
no em dashes anywhere — in copy, AI responses, system prompts. Use a comma, period, or line break instead.
✦
no exclamation marks in AI output — ever. Not excited, not enthusiastic. Warm and present, not performative.
✦
sentence case for all AI output — capitalize the first word of each sentence and proper nouns. No ALL CAPS emphasis. Body copy, FAQs, and general content follow normal sentence case too — only the spoonstack brand name and widget headers stay lowercase.
✦
no clinical language — never "dysregulated," "executive dysfunction," "trauma response," "cognitive overload" in UI copy or AI output. Use the user's own language.
✦
widget headers lowercase — "partner briefing" not "Partner Briefing." Always lowercase in widget UI.
✦
AI responses under ~150 words — restraint is care. Long AI responses overwhelm ND brains. Say less, mean more.
✦
forbidden phrases — "you need to" · "don't forget" · "stay on track" · "complete your" · "I've noticed you tend to." These create anxiety, not support.
✦
no "my kids" as a stack name anywhere — it's "my family."
✦
demo family is Wallace only — Diane, Andre, Simone, Noah, Lily. Real family names never in public-facing content.
the feel

Warm, handmade, ND-brain-native. Not a productivity app. Not clinical. Not corporate. spoonstack speaks the way a really good friend would — someone who remembers things about you, asks good questions, and never makes you feel judged for forgetting. Phase 1 is a whisper. Everything has warmth and restraint. Phase 2 speaks up a bit more — see the Phase 2 style guide.
