"all stacks" pill spec

Lives on the home screen below the 4 pinned tiles. Designed and approved.

Location: app/display/page.tsx — below PinnedWidgets, above SmartInbox
Trigger: pill button labeled "all stacks ↓"
Behavior on tap:
  - Button chevron rotates 180°
  - 8 colored pills appear with staggered animation (40ms delay each)

locked decisions
Do not re-debate these. They are settled.
  • "spoonstack" always lowercase — everywhere, always
  • 4-tab nav: home · people · brain · stacks
  • "while you have a minute" is a subtitle on home screen, not a nav tab
  • out of sight, not out of mind = stack, not nav tab
  • "all stacks" pill on home expands 8 colored pills inline — not a navigation page
  • Button style: neo-B softer (1.5px border, 12px radius, 2px/2px shadow, DM Sans, no all-caps)
  • "got it" not "found it" in where did I put it
  • X-only close on all bottom sheets — no tap-outside-to-dismiss
  • No "save to brain" on partner briefing — auto-logs silently
  • Regulator keeps "save to brain"
  • Ask the vault: "ask a follow-up" + "done" buttons (not "save this answer")
  • Capture bar ambiguous saves → ask clarifying question before saving
  • 29 widgets · 8 stacks — locked count
  • Demo family = Wallace (Diane, Andre, Simone, Noah, Lily)
  • Real family never appears on landing page
  • Forbidden phrases: "you need to" · "don't forget" · "stay on track" · "complete your"
  • No em dashes in app copy or AI responses
  • Landing story cards: stacked vertically on mobile
  • No "my kids" as a stack name anywhere
  • Light theme throughout — dark reserved for capture bar + section headers only
