typography

  • Body, headings, UI: DM Sans — weights 400 / 500 / 700
  • Labels, tags, mono, timestamps: IBM Plex Mono — weights 400 / 500
  • AI responses: sentence case · no em dashes · no exclamation marks · under ~150 words
  • Widget headers: lowercase always

component quick ref

  • Cards: #FFFDF7 bg · 0.5px rgba(44,40,32,.09) border · 13–18px radius · 2.5px color accent bar
  • Buttons: cancel = coral solid pill · confirm = green solid pill · primary = ink pill with gold text
  • Cancel + done buttons: identical shape and weight — never one looks stronger
  • Bottom nav: gold stroke + label + 4px gold dot (active) · ink at 35% opacity (inactive)
  • Capture bar: #1A1714 bg · 14px radius · send = #DAAF2E 26px square 8px radius
